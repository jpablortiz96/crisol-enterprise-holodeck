# Runbook KB-ESC-003

Synthetic demonstration document.

## Purpose

Use this runbook to choose escalation timing and stakeholder communication during a synthetic incident.

## Escalation Triggers

- Revenue-at-risk exceeds 15000 synthetic currency units per hour.
- Two or more critical systems are affected.
- Data consistency, identity integrity, or payment correctness is uncertain.
- The first response team cannot identify a stabilizing action within 15 minutes.

## Communication Standard

Every update should include impact, current hypothesis, action owner, next checkpoint, and open decision.
